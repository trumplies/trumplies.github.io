About The Trump Lie Logs

This is meant to be a single compendium documenting the lies and untruths told by Trump and his administration since he assumed office, alongside sources. This list is bound to be incomplete, but we'll try!
We'll let others do the subtle analysis of The Nature Of Truth needed to really thoroughly fact-check things; we're instead merely trying to put all the administration's mistruths in one place, alongside some sources.
Claims purportedly supported by "evidence" that is vaguely referred to but neither named nor described are assumed to have no evidentiary basis. This is stricter than the burden of proof required of somebody you're chatting with at a bar or at a a baseball game, but we're not at a baseball game.
We highly encourage you to read many sources on these matters! Don't take our word!
